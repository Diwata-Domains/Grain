# Framing — GLP-1 obesity market

## Research question

How large and how durable is the GLP-1 receptor-agonist market for obesity
treatment, and what structural forces will shape it over the next 3–5 years?

## Sub-questions

1. What is the current and projected size of the GLP-1 obesity market? [needs source]
2. Who are the incumbent suppliers and what is their relative position? [needs source]
3. What are the principal demand drivers (prevalence, coverage, awareness)?
4. What are the principal supply constraints (manufacturing, formulation)?
5. What downside risks could compress the market (reimbursement, competition,
   safety signals)? [needs source]

## Scope

- **In scope:** GLP-1 (and dual GIP/GLP-1) agonists used for chronic weight
  management; the commercial dynamics of supply, demand, and access.
- **Out of scope:** type-2 diabetes indications except where they constrain
  obesity supply; clinical-trial design detail; non-incretin obesity drugs.

## Audience and decision

A generalist investment committee deciding whether the obesity-incretin theme
warrants a dedicated position. The brief should inform sizing and timing, not
single-name selection.

## Assumptions and terms

- "GLP-1 obesity market" = revenue attributable to chronic-weight-management use.
- Projections assume no abrupt reimbursement reversal in major markets.
- "Durability" = the market's resistance to compression from competition or
  access limits over a 3–5 year horizon.
