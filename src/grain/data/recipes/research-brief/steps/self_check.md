## Goal

Critically review the draft above against its sources and produce a structured
review with concrete fixes. This step produces a review, not a rewrite.

## Do this

1. Check every claim in the draft against the source list. Flag any claim that
   is unsupported, weakly supported, or still carries a `[needs source]` marker.
2. Check for internal contradictions and unsupported leaps in reasoning.
3. Check that every framed sub-question is actually answered.
4. Check tone, length, and that the bottom line is stated up front.
5. For each issue, record: location, problem, and a specific suggested fix.

Review only what is in the draft and sources above; do not add new research.

## Write the artifact

Write the review to `05-review.md` as a Markdown checklist plus a numbered list
of issues, each with a concrete fix. End with an overall verdict
(ship / revise / block).
