# Step 1 — Frame the topic

You are framing a research brief on:

> {{topic}}

## Goal

Turn the raw topic into a clear, bounded research question with explicit scope.
Do not gather sources yet — this step only frames the work.

## Do this

1. Restate **{{topic}}** as a single, sharp research question.
2. List 3–6 key sub-questions the brief must answer.
3. Define scope: what is in scope and what is explicitly out of scope.
4. State the intended audience and the decision the brief should inform.
5. List the assumptions and any terms that need defining.

Work only from the topic above. Do not assume network access; reason from what
you already know and mark anything that will need a source with `[needs source]`.

## Write the artifact

Write your framing to `01-intake.md`. Use clear Markdown headings for the
research question, sub-questions, scope, audience, and assumptions.
